About Us

We are a dedicated team of educators, developers, and designers passionate about bringing cybersecurity education to the forefront.

Team Mission Statement

We want to teach students about network security using an engaging visual novel and taking real life events to explain the terms and ideas. In doing so, we will help them become more educated individuals in the networking and security realm.

Team Value Proposition

Enemy of the State allows for students to learn more about security and why it is vital in their everyday life. Maintaining an engaging learning environment stimulates comprehension, allowing for the students to learn and have fun at the same time.
